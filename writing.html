Timed Writing

Here Are Some Tips For Writing

Do a quick outline. Don't waste your time trying to cram everything into outline. Write down important things then move on.

Find evidence that you need because that will back up what you're saying. The right evidence helps make your evidence become strong.

Write about what you have an idea on. It's better to write out your thoughts so that you don't forget. Also, write about things that you know because it'll be simpler for you.

Pay attention to key words because that will be an advantage for your writing.

In timed writing, KEEP THINGS SHORT AND SIMPLE. Don't overcomplicate your life by writing varied syntax. Stay safe and keep things short and simple, but that doesn't mean to not expalin. Have your explainations be straight forward.




In Class Essays

Here Are Some Tips For Writing In Class essays

Just like you would for a timed essay, make a quick outline first. It doesn't have t be super deatiled or full sentences.

Also similarly to a timed essay, gather lots of evidence to prove your thesis. Make sure your evidence is strong and relevent to the topic.

Use the key terms from yopur thesis repeatedly throughout your essay. For example, use them in your opening statements of your body paragraphs

Form a deep analysis. This will not only show that you really understand what you're talking about, it will also make your essay much better to read.
